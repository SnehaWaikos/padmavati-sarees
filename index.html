Our Story

A Family Legacy in Every Drape

❋ ✦ ❋
Est. 1962 · Kumbhoj, Kolhapur

What started in 1962 as a modest shop at Adinath Chowk, Kumbhoj has grown into one of Kolhapur district's most trusted names in Indian ethnic wear. Padmavati Sarees & Fashion Corner was founded under the blessings of Goddess Padmavati Devi, and that divine grace has guided every thread, every drape, and every customer relationship across six decades.

Today, the family continues this sacred tradition with the same personal touch that customers have known since 1962 — personally visiting weavers in Varanasi, Kanchipuram, Surat, and Dharmavaram to bring the finest Banarasi, Kanjivaram, and designer sarees at honest prices.

Our physical store at Adinath Chowk, Kumbhoj, Dist. Kolhapur – 416111 welcomes customers six days a week. We believe in building relationships, not just transactions. Every customer who walks through our door becomes a part of the Padmavati family.

Our Journey

Milestones Through the Years

❋ ✦ ❋
1962
The Beginning
Padmavati Sarees & Fashion Corner is founded at Adinath Chowk, Kumbhoj — a humble beginning with a curated selection of Banarasi and cotton sarees that quickly earned the trust of local families.
1985
Growing Reputation
Over two decades in, the store has become a household name in Kumbhoj. Customers travel from across Kolhapur district for bridal sarees and festive collections.
2000
Kanjivaram Arrives
After the first direct trip to Kanchipuram, the store begins stocking authentic Kanjivaram silk sarees. Bridal enquiries double within six months.
2008
Lehenga & Ethnic Wear Expansion
Growing demand from young customers leads to the introduction of Lehenga Cholis and Salwar Kameez sets. The store doubles its floor space.
2015
Second Generation Joins
The next generation of the family joins the business, bringing fresh design sense while preserving the traditional values and personal service the store is known for.
2020
WhatsApp Commerce
During challenging times, the family adapts by offering full catalogue browsing and ordering via WhatsApp. Customers across Maharashtra and beyond start ordering.
2025
63 Years & Still Going Strong
Celebrating over six decades of serving Kumbhoj and Kolhapur. Three generations, thousands of families, and the same passion for authentic Indian weaves that was there on day one in 1962.

Refund & Exchange Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Padmavati Sarees & Fashion Corner, customer satisfaction is our priority. Please read our exchange and refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. 7-Day Exchange Policy

  • Eligible products purchased in-store or via WhatsApp/online order can be exchanged within 7 days of purchase or delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, with original tags and packaging intact, and accompanied by the original purchase receipt or order confirmation.
  • Exchanges are subject to availability of alternate stock. If an exact replacement is unavailable, store credit may be offered.

2. Non-Returnable / Non-Exchangeable Items

  • Custom-stitched or tailored items (blouses, custom orders) made to specific measurements.
  • Items purchased on final clearance/sale that are explicitly marked "non-returnable."
  • Products that show signs of use, alteration, perfume, or damage caused after delivery.

3. Refunds

  • We primarily offer exchange or store credit. Monetary refunds are considered only in case of damaged, defective, or incorrect items delivered, verified within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Approved refunds are processed to the original mode of payment within 7–10 business days.
  • Shipping charges (if any) are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

4. How to Request an Exchange/Refund

  • Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 73784 31843 or email padmavatiareesfc@gmail.com within the applicable window.
  • Share your order details, photos of the product (if defective/damaged), and reason for exchange/return.
  • Our team will guide you on the next steps, including return shipping (if applicable) or in-store exchange.

5. Order Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch by contacting us directly. Once an order is shipped or custom tailoring has begun, cancellation may not be possible.

For any queries regarding this policy, please reach out to us at our store, Adinath Chowk, Kumbhoj, Dist. Kolhapur – 416111, or via the contact details listed on our Contact Us page.

Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Padmavati Sarees & Fashion Corner ships products across India via trusted courier partners. This policy outlines how your order is processed, packed, and delivered after purchase.

1. Shipping Coverage

  • We offer Pan-India shipping for all products ordered via WhatsApp, phone, or email.
  • International shipping is not currently available. For inquiries, please contact us directly on WhatsApp.
  • In-store pickup is also available at our Kumbhoj store — no shipping charges apply for pickups.

2. Order Processing Time

  • In-stock items are dispatched within 1–2 business days of order confirmation and payment.
  • Custom-stitched or made-to-order items (blouses, custom lengths) require 3–5 working days before dispatch.
  • Orders placed during festive seasons (Navratri, Diwali, wedding season) may take slightly longer — we will communicate any delay proactively.

3. Delivery Timelines

  • Within Maharashtra: 2–4 business days after dispatch.
  • Rest of India: 4–8 business days after dispatch.
  • Remote / Tier-3 areas: 7–12 business days after dispatch.
  • Timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier delays, public holidays, or force majeure events beyond our control.

4. Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges, if applicable, will be communicated to you before dispatch and are based on the weight, size, and destination of the order.
  • For orders above a certain value (communicated at the time of ordering), we may offer free or subsidised shipping as a goodwill gesture.
  • In-store pickup is always free.

5. Packaging

  • All sarees and ethnic wear are carefully folded, tissue-wrapped, and packed in secure boxes or bags to prevent damage in transit.
  • Bridal lehengas and heavy silk sarees are packed with extra padding and a sealed protective cover.
  • We take pride in elegant, gift-worthy packaging — especially for bridal or gifted pieces. Please let us know if you'd like a special gift note included.

6. Courier Partners

We work with reputed courier partners including DTDC, Delhivery, Bluedart, and India Post (for remote areas) to ensure safe and timely delivery. A tracking number is shared via WhatsApp or SMS once your order is dispatched.

7. Risk & Title

Risk of loss and title for products pass to you upon delivery to the courier/shipping partner. In case of loss or damage in transit, please contact us immediately so we can coordinate with the courier on your behalf.

8. Undelivered / Returned Shipments

  • If a shipment is returned to us due to an incorrect address, failed delivery attempts, or refusal to accept, reshipping charges may apply.
  • Please ensure your delivery address and phone number are accurate when placing an order.

9. Contact for Shipping Queries

For any shipping-related questions, please contact us on WhatsApp at +91 73784 31843 or email padmavatiareesfc@gmail.com. We typically respond within a few hours during business hours (Mon–Sat 10am–8pm).
